Outcomes or Bust
Output is meaningless without delivering
outcomes to the customer that impact their
mission. We are purpose-driven, dedicated
to serving something beyond our own self-
interests.
Keep it Real
Real working software, rapidly deployed | to
real operations environments | to solve real
problems | for real users | to create real

Learn the Fastest
We move fast so we can innovate
through experimentation and learn faster.
We’re less afraid of failure than we are of
losing opportunities or the chance for
feedback by moving too slowly.
Be Less Certain
You can’t learn the fastest if you listen the
slowest. Prod is the ultimate arbiter of truth,
not your resume. Step outside your ego and
empathize with others.
Prodacity
Be willing to take bold risks to get to prod and
even more willing to learn from it. Empower
yourself and everyone around you to make
deliberate, autonomous decisions.
